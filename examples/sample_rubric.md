# Grading Rubric: Temperature Converter

## Requirements Match (30 points)
- celsius_to_fahrenheit function: 10 points
- fahrenheit_to_celsius function: 10 points
- User input and output handling: 5 points
- main() function: 5 points

## Correctness (30 points)
- Conversion formulas are correct: 15 points
- Program runs without errors: 10 points
- Handles invalid input: 5 points

## Code Quality (20 points)
- Clear variable/function naming: 7 points
- Code organization and structure: 7 points
- Comments and docstrings: 6 points

## Output & Completeness (20 points)
- Output matches expected format: 10 points
- Program is complete and runnable: 10 points

## Total: 100 points

### Grade Scale
- A: 90-100
- B: 80-89
- C: 70-79
- D: 60-69
- F: 0-59
